        <h2>
          Ingredients
          </h2>
          <ul>
            <li>Stove</li>
            <li>Frying pan</li>
            <li>Egg</li>
            <li>Oil</li>
            </ul>
            <h2>
              Instructions
              </h2>
              <ol>
                <li>First turn the stove on</li>
                <li>Then put oil in the frying pan</li>
                <li>Then crack the egg and pour it in the frying pan</li>
                <li>flip the egg once the other side is fried</li>
                <li>when both sides are equally fried take it off the fryimg pan and now you have an omlette</li>
